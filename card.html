Twitter

Is a popular micro-blogging and social networking platform. Users communicate through short, real-time posts known as tweets, which appear on the feeds (timelines) of people who follow their accounts


Instagram

Instagram is a popular social media and networking platform dedicated to sharing photos, short videos (Reels), and disappearing updates (Stories). The name itself is a combination of two words: "Instant" (referring to instant cameras) and "Telegram"

Youtube

YouTube is a free, global online video-sharing platform owned by Google. It allows users to watch, upload, share, comment on, and interact with video content ranging from entertainment and education to live streams and short-form videos (Shorts).
